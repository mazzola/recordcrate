We find the bands.

Once we find a bunch of awesome new bands, we put their latests albums into a crate. Think of crates as just a big bundle of digital music joy. You can get your crate in MP3, FLAC, or whatever.

We give it to you!

Once you open up your crate, we know you'll find something you'll love. Plus you're directly supporting the up and coming artists create more incredible music.
